How a Mural Commission Works

01

Consultation

We talk about your space, your vision, and your budget. You share photos of the wall. I share ideas for what could live there. No commitment at this stage.

02

Concept

I create initial sketches based on our conversation. You see the vision before any paint touches the wall. We refine together until it feels right.

03

Painting

I work on-site, transforming the space. The process itself becomes part of the story. Clients often tell me their customers stop to watch.

04

Completion

Your space is transformed. The mural becomes a landmark, a conversation piece, a daily reminder that your environment carries intention.

Ready to Transform Your Space?

Every mural begins with a conversation. Tell me about your space and what you're imagining. No commitment, no pressure. Just two people talking about what's possible.
