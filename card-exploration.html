Dark Temple: Card Display Exploration

How Should the Cards Feel?

Same Dark Temple palette. Same paintings. Five different ways to present the product cards, each exploring how to make a digital card feel like a physical tarot card you want to pick up.

Hover over each card to see the interaction. The one that feels right becomes the product card for the entire shop.

All variations use real art from Sarah's site · Dark Temple colors · Cormorant Garamond + Lora + Raleway
Option 1

The Sacred Frame

A gold inset border frames the painting like a gilded picture frame in a dark gallery. The warm info panel below grounds it. Rounded corners soften the edges. On hover, the whole frame lifts and a warm gold glow appears.

  • Rounded corners (12px outer, 6px image)
  • Thin gold border around painting
  • Warm panel below for name + info
  • Hover: lift + gold glow shadow
  • Inspired by Concept B's padded image area

The Floating Altar

The card levitates above the dark surface. A dramatic drop shadow creates the illusion of floating. On hover, it tilts in 3D, like picking a card up off the table. The info overlays the bottom of the image through a gradient fade, so the card itself is one continuous object.

  • Rounded corners (14px)
  • Deep, layered shadow — the card floats
  • 3D tilt on hover (perspective transform)
  • Gradient overlay — info fades in over painting
  • Full-bleed image — no separate info panel
